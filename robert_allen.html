12 ways to increase the number of leads:
   1. referrals
   2. first order at cost
   3. guarantee or risk reversal
   4. host/beneficiary relationship
   5. advertising
   6. direct mail
   7. telemarketing
   8. special events and seminars
   9. use qualified list
  10. USP
  11. client education
  12. PR (public relations)

9 ways to increase your closing ration (convert leads into buying
customers):
   1. increase selling skills of your entire stuff.
   2. listen to best books and tapes
   3. model your best sales people
   4. get a sales manager
   5. go out and get sold - model your competitors
   6. qualify leads up front
   7. make ads specific - not general
   8. make irresistable offers
   9. educate your customers

3 ways to increase retention and decrease attrition:
   1. call inactive customers to get feedback
   2. give extraordinary service
   3. communicate

6 ways to increase the size of your average order:
   1. up-sell and cross-sell
   2. point-of-purchase promotion
   3. bundling/packaging of complimentary products together.
   4. increase your prices and enhance your margin
   5. change the profile of your products to be more upscale
   6. offer larger units of purchase

6 ways to increase the frequency of purchase:
   1. develop a backend
   2. continuos communication
   3. endorse other people's products to your list
   4. run special/private/closed door sales
   5. program your customers; educate them into a long-term buying strategy
   6. use price/purchase inducements like frequent-flyer miles
