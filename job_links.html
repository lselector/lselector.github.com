Job-search links.
 
 
Below are some links related to job search (and executive job search).  I actually never had a chance to use these sites because I was finding the jobs very easily.  But you may find them useful if you are looking for your first job or plan to relocate.

There are estimated 100,000 sites related to job hunting.  They serve several functions: job listings, resume listings, career counselling, research on fields, occupations, companies, cities, etc., useful contacts.
